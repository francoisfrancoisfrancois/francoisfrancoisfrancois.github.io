Design and layout of a 118-page French-Russian edition of The Aims of Astronautics by Konstantin Tsiolkovsky, Russian pioneer of astronautical theory and visionary of space exploration. The book combines the text of The Goals of Astronautics with Tsiolkovsky's later illustrations for his Album of Cosmic Travels, revealing a poetic and avant-garde vision of space travel and life in zero gravity. wwww.nice.rocks
Type
Website, Identity
Client
Self-initiated
Year
2025
Design and creation of a personal website featuring a digital collection of stones collected during walks, a childhood pastime revisited on a digital scale. Each stone, geolocated and documented in situ without being taken away, finds its place in a mosaic structured by a grid. Dividers allow the images to be grouped according to the location and date of each walk. Autobahn

Exhibition, Communication
Client
HelloMe /Jörg Brüggemann
Year
2021
Exhibition design, signage and communication materials (posters, posts and stories) for Autobahn, a solo exhibition by photographer Jörg Brüggemann at La Friche la Belle de Mai in Marseille, France (16 July – 22 August 2021). The exhibition explores the German motorway network, its landscapes, its dreams of freedom and the lives of those who live and work around the motorway. Conception de l’exposition, de sa signalétique et de ses supports de communication pour Autobahn de Joerg Brüggemann, présentée à la Friche la Belle de Mai, explorant le réseau autoroutier allemand et la vie qui l’entoure. Exhibition design / Signage and visual identity / Graphic design / Digital design / Image editing and processing
